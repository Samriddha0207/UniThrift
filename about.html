The Problem We Solved

Every semester, students spend thousands on textbooks, lab equipment, and hostel essentials — and then sell them at a fraction of the price at the end of the year, to strangers, with zero trust or safety. Facebook groups were chaotic. WhatsApp forwards got lost. There was no student-first marketplace built for Indian campus life.

So we built one.

What UniThrift Does

  • Verified student-only listings — no random strangers
  • Real-time chat between buyers and sellers
  • AI-assisted document verification for sellers (PAN + College ID)
  • Notifications when someone messages you or your item sells
  • Campus-local discovery — see what's near you first
